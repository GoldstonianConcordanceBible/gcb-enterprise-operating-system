# Contribution-Only Policy

This governance system is structured for contributions, review, and participation.

- No investment framing
- No profit promises
- No ROI language
- Contributions may be recognized through access, attribution, and participation status

## What is a contribution?
- Canon edits, schema improvements, documentation improvements
- Reviews and issue triage
- Translations, dataset work, indexing work

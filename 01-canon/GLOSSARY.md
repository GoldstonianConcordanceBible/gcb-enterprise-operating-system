# Glossary (Canonical)

## Mirror
Identity, motive, posture, covenant alignment, and reflection.

## Water
Discernment, cleansing, evidence filtering, pattern recognition.

## Fire
Refinement, judgment, transformation, legitimate authority.

## Canon
The officially maintained body of primary works and authoritative specifications.

## Canonicality
A work’s position in the hierarchy: primary, supporting, commentary, draft.

## Resolver
A deterministic rule system that maps aliases/identifiers to canonical records.

## Contribution-only governance
Participation model focused on contributions, review, and access—not investment or ROI.
